This game serves as an interactive demonstration of a Variable Ratio Schedule (VR) within the study of Operant Conditioning in psychology.

In psychology, a Variable Ratio Schedule is a schedule of reinforcement where a response is reinforced after an unpredictable number of responses. This schedule creates a high, steady rate of responding with a high resistance to extinction—similar to how slot machines and lottery systems keep individuals on their toes!

To learn more about the mechanics of conditioning and reinforcement, visit the Wikipedia page on Reinforcement .
