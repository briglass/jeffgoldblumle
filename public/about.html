Meet the Creator

Independent Developer & Word Game Enthusiast

Hello! I'm an independent developer and fan of creative word games. I built Jeffgoldblumle to offer a unique, lighthearted spin on the daily word-puzzle format for players around the world.

🦖 Daily Routine & Novelty

My goal is to provide a novel type of Wordle clone for people's daily routine—something fun, free, and slightly absurd to enjoy with your morning coffee. By mixing the classic 5-letter guessing mechanics with a heavy dose of Jeff Goldblum-themed vocab and references, I hope to add a brief, smile-inducing puzzle to your everyday schedule.
